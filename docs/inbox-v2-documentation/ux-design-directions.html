Evaluation

✓ Pros

  • No modal navigation - stay in context
  • Collapsed view shows all critical info
  • Inline editing feels fast and lightweight
  • Matches established Intercom pattern
  • Easy to scan multiple triggers
  • Progressive disclosure hides complexity

✗ Cons

  • Expanded view pushes content down (page jump)
  • Limited vertical space for long lists
  • May feel constrained on smaller screens

Evaluation

✓ Pros

  • Maximum triggers visible at once (scanning efficiency)
  • Less scrolling for power users with 10+ triggers
  • Quick status overview at a glance
  • Efficient use of vertical space

✗ Cons

  • Less breathing room - feels cramped
  • Smaller text may reduce readability
  • Not suitable for new users (overwhelming)
  • Less space for contextual information
  • May not meet accessibility touch targets (44px)

Evaluation

✓ Pros

  • Strong calendar metaphor - instantly understandable
  • Visual preview of when messages send (dates)
  • Familiar pattern from Google/Apple Calendar
  • Can show existing sends on calendar (if needed)

✗ Cons

  • Takes significant vertical space
  • Overkill for simple weekly patterns (every Thursday)
  • Monthly view may confuse recurring weekly patterns
  • Complex to implement for "every Thursday" vs specific dates
  • Not suitable for weekday-based patterns (M-F)

✓ Pros

  • Fewer triggers visible at once (more scrolling)
  • Minimal status indicators may reduce confidence
