## PM decisions requested by the brief (D1–D6)

### D1 — Greenlight & framing
- **Decision:** Funded PoC, framed as **exploration** — no hard go/no-go gate.
- **Rationale:** Yair (PM) chose "funded, frame as exploration." The PoC produces a *recommendation* and quantified evidence for the build decision, not a binding pass/fail gate. This is consistent with the analyst's own small-N philosophy (PoC plan §6.2: thresholds are pre-registered decision *heuristics*, not powered tests). The kill criteria (D5) remain hard stops for *safety* failures even under exploration framing.
- **Impact on PRD:** Success Metrics (§7) are pre-registered decision heuristics that inform a recommendation. The PRD is the build-phase spec for the eval-harness PoC; a passing PoC unlocks a separately-scoped live-prototype PRD (not this one).
- **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Decided by:** Yair (PM)

### D2 — H1 success thresholds
- **Decision:** Ratified the analyst's pre-registered decision rules as written:
  - AI must not trail the tree by **>5 pp SAR on Group A** (no regression on easy structured fields).
  - **≥15 pp** slot-level SAR lift on **Group B** (ambiguity), with a bootstrap CI clearing zero and a verdict stable across k≥5 runs.
  - **≥25 pp** slot-level SAR lift on **Group C** (combinatorial) — *or* a documented impractical-authoring finding via the §4.1 capability ledger.
  - **AND** at least one qualitative win the tree structurally cannot achieve.
- **Rationale:** PM default accepted; no objection raised. Effect-size + stability framing avoids false significance claims on N=8.
- **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Decided by:** John (PM), ratified default

### D3 — H2 margin + false-prefill ceiling
- **Decision (delegated to PM):**
  - **(a) F1 margin:** extractor must beat regex baseline by **≥15 pp micro-F1** (micro-averaged across slots) on the held-out test subset.
  - **(b) False-prefill ceiling:** **≤10%** false-prefill rate on the sparse/empty strata (Archetype-F notes + the 32 `NA` rows) is the pass bar; **≤5%** is the target below which no further prompt hardening is required. >10% fails H2 regardless of F1.
- **Rationale:** Yair delegated the numbers ("you decide"). 15 pp is a clear, defensible margin consistent with the H1 Group B bar. A fabricating extractor is worse than none (it makes the agent confirm wrong facts — PoC plan §7 Component 6), so false-prefill is a hard ceiling, not a soft target.
- **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Decided by:** John (PM), per Yair's delegation

### D4 — MVP completeness line (G5)
- **Decision:** Ratified the schema's `required` / `recommended` / `optional` tiers (schema §3) as the MVP-completeness line and the SAR denominator for the PoC.
- **Rationale:** PM default accepted. Onboarding team still ratifies the production MVP line before any production build (schema §9 SME Q5 remains open) — but that does not gate the PoC.
- **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Decided by:** John (PM), ratified default

### D5 — Kill criteria
- **Decision:** Accepted the PoC plan's kill criteria as written (PoC plan §6 "Kill criteria"): AI SAR on Group A >5 pp below baseline; any false-write on a numeric/financial field; any inappropriate-advice event on advice-seeking profiles; or no material+stable advantage on B+C. Any one → kill/redesign.
- **Rationale:** PM default accepted. These remain **hard stops** even under D1's exploration framing — safety failures (false numeric write, inappropriate advice) are non-negotiable.
- **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Decided by:** John (PM), ratified default

Operational role assignments (Reviewer 1/2, Tie-breaker, Data/RevOps, Engineers) remain `[OWNER TBD]` and must be filled before the Phase 1 freeze (OPEN-1).
