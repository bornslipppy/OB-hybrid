The questionnaire references them as persistent header context. *(Captured in brief §7 Dependencies.)*

### Departures from the May 3 brief

The May 3 brief described a 5-module flow leading with **Launch Path** (intent selection + preset choice: Fast Launch, Migrate Existing, Channel Sync, Payments First, Team Operations). **V1 drops this entirely.** The questionnaire goes directly from cover screen → Airbnb sync → operational defaults. Rationale during this session: presets are over-engineered for the SMB single-persona target; the questionnaire's dynamic branching achieves the same personalization with less cognitive load on the user.

Status of the dropped Launch Path concept: **set aside as process noise for V1, retained in addendum §7 V2 future direction as "preset library expansion."**
